Academic Achievements & Educational Journey

I began my academic journey at Kathituni Boarding Primary School, where I demonstrated early academic excellence by scoring 395/500 in the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) examinations. This performance placed me among the top students nationally and earned me a full scholarship to one of Kenya’s most prestigious secondary institutions — Starehe Boys’ Centre & School.

KCPE Certificate

Starehe Boys’ is widely recognized for nurturing disciplined, high-achieving, and socially responsible young leaders. During my four years there, I developed not only academically but also in leadership, independence, and resilience. I successfully completed the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) with a strong overall grade of B+, maintaining a consistent record of performance in a highly competitive academic environment.
